Additional Sign Materials

Premium sign-making materials including Dibond ACM, Sintra PVC, clear lamination, and LED strip lighting for all your signage projects.

We offer a range of professional sign-making materials to complement any project. From aluminum composite panels to high-intensity LED lighting, we have the materials you need.

Dibond (ACM) — Aluminum Composite Material

Aluminum composite — two sheets of .010" aluminum with thermoplastic core. Impact & fire resistant, waterproof, lightweight. Stocked in 4'×8', 4'×10', 4'×12', 5'×10', 5'×12' sheets.

Sintra (PVC) — Foamed PVC Board

Foamed closed cell PVC. Excellent strength-to-weight ratio. Available in 4'×8' sheets in 3mm and 6mm thicknesses. Colors: Beige, Black, Blue, Dark Blue, Dark Gray, Green, Light Gray, Red, White, Yellow.

Clear Lamination

UV protection up to 4 years. Clear adhesive back that won't distort image quality. Ideal for vehicle graphics, front-lit signage, retail displays, and large graphic applications.

LED Strip Lighting

Flexible, high-intensity LED strips. Waterproof and color-changing options available. Perfect for store fronts, cove lighting, interior lighting, exhibit lighting, and cabinet lighting.
