Dibond (ACM) — Aluminum Composite Material

Aluminum composite material — two sheets of .010" aluminum with a thermoplastic core. Impact resistant, fire resistant, waterproof, and lightweight. The industry standard for professional outdoor signage.

  • 4'×10' sheets

  • 5'×10' sheets
  • 5'×12' sheets

Sintra (PVC) — Foamed PVC Board

Foamed closed-cell PVC with excellent strength-to-weight ratio. Ideal for indoor and outdoor signage, displays, and point-of-purchase applications. Available in 4'×8' sheets.
